Design of Diabetic Shoes.

“

Our product offers a versatile design and delivers a highly customized product with aesthetic value while solving issues caused by diabetes is a priority.

We chose to design shoes for patients who suffer from diabetes and to offer a customized solution as every patient has different anatomy and needs. By using 3D scanning, Computational design tools, and 3D printing we can achieve a high level of shoe customization and production delivering an optimal product built to customer’s needs.

As for Design for All principles, a product designed for people with specific needs can benefit the entire community.
