About me.

I am a PhD student at the University of Western Macedonia.
I am involved in various projects related to design and problem solving ideas. By following a holistic design process and creative tools, I can find a satisfactory solution to the needs of the customer. Furthermore, with the assistance of digital sketching, 3D programs and rendering, I am able to present the final image efficiently and prototype it.

In my leisure time I enjoy making crafts, sketching and drawing, improving my skills in photography and coding, studying robotics, learning new skills and overall exploring different creative paths.
