About me.

Ioanna Kalamakidou is currently a PhD candidate at the University of Western Macedonia.
Meanwhile, she is involved in various projects related to design and problem-solving. By following a holistic design process and using creative tools, she is able to identify and develop solutions that effectively meet clients' needs. Furthermore, with the assistance of digital sketching, 3D programs and rendering techniques, she can efficiently present final concepts and develop prototypes.


In her leisure time, Ioanna Kalamakidou enjoys making crafts, sketching and drawing, improving her skills in photography and coding, studying robotics, learning new skills and overall exploring different creative paths.
