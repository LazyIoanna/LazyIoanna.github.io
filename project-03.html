Packaging Design for Sparkling Water.

“

Our goal was to design a 330ml sleek aluminum can for sparkling water, that will be aesthetcally minimal and able to attract the end-user/customer, environmental friendly and take advantage of the aluminum as a popular manufacturing material.

We introduced Artficial Intelligence tools to our project, such as image generators in order to expand our inspiraton.

The main element of the design is the bird "Egret". Egrets are the symbol of elegance, purity, peace and harmony, which are some of the objectives of our aluminum can packaging.
